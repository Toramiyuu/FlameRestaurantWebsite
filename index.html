Our Story

Penang's First
Open Flame Dining

Perched high atop De View Hotel in Ayer Itam, Flame is a rooftop restaurant that redefines dining with a view. Every dish is a celebration of smoky aromas and flame-grilled perfection, crafted over an open fire with carefully sourced ingredients.


From aged steaks to fresh seafood, our kitchen delivers bold flavours against a stunning backdrop of Kek Lok Si Temple and the Penang Hill skyline. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a memorable meal, Flame is where the evening comes alive.
